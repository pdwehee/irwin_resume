Get To Know Me

Hello! I'm Irwin Y. Lue, a dedicated and results-driven finance and banking professional with over 15 years of experience at the Liberian Bank for Development and Investment.

I hold a Master's degree in International Banking and Finance from Bangor University, UK, where I graduated with Merit. I have a proven track record of leading financial initiatives that promote economic growth and sustainable development. I am committed to implementing innovative financial practices aligned with sustainable development goals.

I actively foster financial inclusion for all Liberians and support the Liberian Development Agenda. My robust analytical skills, coupled with strong dedication to social impact, uniquely position me to contribute effectively to governmental financial strategies and policies.

Additional Information

Accessing investment opportunities for African and Liberian farmers and businesses has always been a core passion of mine as an investment banker in Liberia.

Over the past fifteen years, I have traveled extensively across rural Liberia, conducting assessments of farms and small businesses. These visits have deepened my passion for investment banking as I witnessed the urgent need for financial opportunities to empower farmers and entrepreneurs across Africa.

My principal objective is to facilitate investment opportunities enabling African farmers and businesses to grow—moving from subsistence farming to commercial agriculture—and unlocking sustainable income potential. I am committed to creating a safe, reliable economic environment that attracts local and international investors to Sub-Saharan Africa. This can help generate jobs and alleviate poverty hampering growth and development.

Throughout my tenure at the Liberian Bank for Development and Investment, I have remained dedicated to ensuring farmers and small businesses gain access to crucial investment opportunities. To further this mission, I aspire to acquire education that enables me to drive a vibrant and thriving socioeconomic landscape in Liberia and Africa at large.
